PROGRAM_OVERVIEW:
The Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program (formerly Microsoft Cloud Partner Program, as of Oct 2022) replaced the legacy 18 Gold and Silver competencies with six **Solutions Partner** designations, each aligned to a specific high-demand cloud solution area:
- Data & AI (Azure)
- Infrastructure (Azure)
- Digital & App Innovation (Azure)
- Modern Work
- Business Applications
- Security

GLOBAL_REQUIREMENTS:
- **PROGRAM_ENROLLMENT:** Must be an active member of the Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program with a valid Partner Center account.
- **PARTNER_CAPABILITY_SCORE (PCS):** Must achieve at least **70 out of 100 points** in the chosen solution area to attain the designation.
- **METRIC_MINIMUM_POINTS:** Must earn **more than 0 points in every individual metric** category (no metric can remain at 0 points).
- **SCORING_CATEGORIES:** Points are measured automatically in Partner Center across three categories – **Performance, Skilling, and Customer Success** – using key metrics recorded from partner activities and achievements.

TRACK_CLASSIFICATION:
- **Azure Solution Areas (Data & AI, Infrastructure, Digital & App Innovation):** A partner is classified as **SMB track** if their total **Azure Consumed Revenue (ACR)** over the last 12 months is **< USD 1,000,000** **AND** **> 80%** of their customers are in the SMB segment. Otherwise, the partner is on the **Enterprise track**. (ACR is calculated from CSP direct & indirect, Digital Partner of Record (DPOR), and Partner Admin Link (PAL) associations.)
- **Business Applications:** Partner is classified as Enterprise track if they have **≥ 5 unique workloads** that each contribute **> USD 5,000 in revenue per customer tenant** in both month (current month–1) and month (current month–13). Otherwise, track classification is SMB.
- **Modern Work:** Partner performance is automatically **evaluated on both Enterprise and SMB paths** for each metric. The partner’s overall score for Modern Work is based on the **higher scoring path** (Enterprise vs. SMB) at the **solution area level** (not on a per-metric basis).
- **Security:** Similar to Modern Work, partner performance is evaluated on **both tracks** (Azure-based vs. Microsoft 365-based engagement) and the combination that yields the higher overall Security score will be used. 

QUALIFICATION_WINDOW_AND_RENEWAL:
- **NEW_PARTNER_ELIGIBILITY_WINDOW:** After purchasing a Solutions Partner designation, **new partners** have a qualification window spanning the **current month + the previous 5 months** to achieve Qualified status (≥ 70 points, all metrics > 0 at least once) in the target solution area.
- **RENEWAL_WINDOW_EXISTING_PARTNERS:** **Existing Solutions Partners** (already holding a designation) must qualify annually by achieving ≥ 70 points with no zero metrics during the 6-month window ending in their **anniversary (renewal) month**. Designations are valid for 1 year from attainment and must be renewed each year.
- **CROSS_ENROLLMENT:** If a partner already holds a Solutions Partner designation in one area and subsequently achieves Qualified status in another area during their eligibility window, they are **automatically enrolled** in the new solution area’s designation **without additional purchase**.
- **RENEWAL_GRACE_PERIOD:** There is a **30-day renewal grace period** following the anniversary date. If a partner fails to requalify within that window, the designation lapses and a new purchase/enrollment is required to regain it.
- **GOVERNMENT_CUSTOMERS:** Government/sovereign cloud customers in **public/commercial** cloud environments are **included** in partner scoring. Government customers in non-public clouds (e.g., US Gov, China) are **excluded** from scoring metrics.

SOLUTION_AREA: Data & AI (Azure)
PREREQUISITES: Active MAICPP enrollment; ≥ 2 individuals with **Azure Administrator Associate** certification (prerequisite for intermediate skilling points); ≥ 2 individuals with **Azure Solutions Architect Expert** certification (prerequisite for advanced skilling points).
METRIC_NAME: Performance — Net Customer Adds
DESCRIPTION: Each net-new Azure customer (meeting eligibility criteria) contributes 10 points towards the Partner Capability Score.
MAX_POINTS: 30
CALCULATION: Net new customers added = (Eligible customers at end of last month) − (Eligible customers 12 months ago).
THRESHOLDS_BY_TRACK: Enterprise – customer tenant must have ≥ USD 1,000 ACR in at least one of the last 2 months to count as eligible; SMB – customer tenant must have ≥ USD 500 ACR in at least one of the last 2 months.
ELIGIBLE_WORKLOADS: All Azure “Service Level 2” services (all Azure services eligible; list subject to change).
ELIGIBLE_OFFERS: All offers except Support, Internal, Benefit (Partner) Program, and Trial offers.
ELIGIBLE_ASSOCIATION_TYPES: CSP (Direct Tier 1 & Indirect Tier 2), Digital Partner of Record (DPOR), Partner Admin Link (PAL).
METRIC_NAME: Skilling — Intermediate Certifications
DESCRIPTION: Awards points for intermediate (associate-level) certifications held by employees, reflecting the partner’s technical expertise. **NOTE:** Uses a two-step prerequisite model.
MAX_POINTS: 20
PREREQUISITES: ≥ 2 individuals must hold **Azure Administrator Associate** certification (Step 1 prerequisite; yields 0 points on its own, but required to earn any intermediate certification points).
SCORING_RULES: Once prerequisite is met, each qualified individual with a **Data & AI** associate-level certification yields 4 points; up to 5 individuals count (5 × 4 = 20 points). Individuals are counted once each (holding multiple associate certs still counts as one individual).
QUALIFYING_CERTIFICATIONS: Azure Database Administrator Associate; Azure AI Engineer Associate; Azure Data Scientist Associate; Azure Data Engineer Associate; Microsoft Certified: Data Analyst Associate (Power BI).
METRIC_NAME: Skilling — Advanced Certifications
DESCRIPTION: Awards points for advanced (expert or specialty-level) certifications held by employees. **NOTE:** Uses a two-step prerequisite model.
MAX_POINTS: 20
PREREQUISITES: ≥ 2 individuals must hold **Azure Solutions Architect Expert** certification (Step 1 prerequisite; required to earn any advanced certification points).
SCORING_RULES: Once prerequisite is met, each qualified individual with a **Data & AI** expert or specialty certification yields 4 points; up to 5 individuals count (5 × 4 = 20 points). Individuals can be the same or different from those counted in Intermediate, but only counted once per metric.
QUALIFYING_CERTIFICATIONS: Customer Data Platform Specialty; Azure Cosmos DB Developer Specialty.
METRIC_NAME: Customer Success — Usage Growth
DESCRIPTION: Measures growth in aggregated Azure Consumed Revenue (ACR) for Azure workloads under management.
MAX_POINTS: 20
CALCULATION: **Percentage ACR growth** = [(Total qualifying ACR last month − Total qualifying ACR in same month 12 months prior) ÷ (Total ACR 12 months prior)] × 100.
THRESHOLD: Partner’s total qualifying ACR must be ≥ USD 1,000 (baseline) for usage growth to be scored.
ELIGIBLE_WORKLOADS: All Azure “Service Level 2” services.
ELIGIBLE_ASSOCIATION_TYPES: CSP (Tier 1, Tier 2); Digital Partner of Record (DPOR); Partner Admin Link (PAL).
METRIC_NAME: Customer Success — Deployments
DESCRIPTION: Measures the number of distinct Azure service **deployments** (unique Azure workloads) that the partner’s customers actively used (with >0 ACR) in the past 12 months.
MAX_POINTS: 10
SCORING_RULES: 2 points per distinct qualifying Azure service deployed; maximum of 5 new deployments (5 × 2 = 10 points).
ELIGIBLE_WORKLOADS: All Azure “Service Level 2” services except **Virtual Machines** and **Virtual Machine Licenses** (these two are excluded from counting).
NOTE: Sum of max points across all metrics in Data & AI = 30 (Performance) + 20 (Intermediate Skilling) + 20 (Advanced Skilling) + 20 (Usage Growth) + 10 (Deployments) = 100 total points.
SPECIALIZATIONS (Optional): Partners who attain the Data & AI designation can pursue advanced specializations such as **Analytics on Microsoft Azure**, **AI and Machine Learning on Microsoft Azure**, **Data Warehouse Migration to Microsoft Azure**, **Build and Modernize AI Apps with Microsoft Azure**, **Infra and Database Migration to Microsoft Azure**, **Kubernetes on Microsoft Azure**, **Migrate Enterprise Apps to Microsoft Azure**. Up to **5** such specializations tied to Azure solution areas can receive additional benefits.

MAX_POINTS: 20
PREREQUISITES: ≥ 2 individuals with **Azure Administrator Associate** (Step 1; required).
SCORING_RULES: Each qualifying person with an **Infrastructure** associate-level certification = 4 points; max 5 persons for full 20 points.
QUALIFYING_CERTIFICATIONS: Azure Network Engineer Associate; Azure Stack Hub Operator Associate; Windows Server Hybrid Administrator Associate _(retired July 31, 2023; qualifies until July 2024)_.
METRIC_NAME: Skilling — Advanced Certifications
DESCRIPTION: Awards points for advanced (expert/specialty-level) certifications (two-step model).
MAX_POINTS: 20
PREREQUISITES: ≥ 2 individuals with **Azure Solutions Architect Expert** (required).
SCORING_RULES: Each qualifying person with an **Infrastructure** expert or specialty certification = 4 points; max 5 persons for full 20 points.
QUALIFYING_CERTIFICATIONS: Azure Virtual Desktop Specialty; Azure for SAP Workloads Specialty.
SCORING_NOTES: For both Intermediate and Advanced certification metrics – examples:
- If exactly 2 people complete prerequisite (Step 1) and 2 different people hold qualifying certifications (Step 2), score = 2 × 4 = 8 points.
- If 2 people fulfill both Step 1 and Step 2 (same two individuals hold prerequisite and qualifying certs), score = 2 × 4 = 8 points.
- If 1 person has met the prerequisite (Step 1) and 2 other people hold qualifying certs (Step 2) but only 1 person has the prerequisite, no points are earned until a second person meets the prerequisite. Once the second person achieves the prerequisite, the 2 qualifying individuals from Step 2 yield 2 × 4 = 8 points.

MAX_POINTS: 20

ELIGIBLE_WORKLOADS: All Azure infrastructure service workloads (Service Level 2 services).
ELIGIBLE_ASSOCIATION_TYPES: CSP (T1, T2); DPOR; PAL.
METRIC_NAME: Customer Success — Deployments
DESCRIPTION: Number of new Azure infrastructure service deployments (unique services) with active usage by customers.
MAX_POINTS: 10
SCORING_RULES: 2 points per new Azure infrastructure service deployment; max 5 deployments for full 10 points (excluding Virtual Machines and VM Licenses).

SOLUTION_AREA: Modern Work
PREREQUISITES: Active program enrollment in MAICPP. (Minimum 1 intermediate and 1 advanced certified individual required to avoid a zero in one of the skilling sub-metrics.)
METRIC_NAME: Performance — Net Customer Adds
DESCRIPTION: Measures growth of customer base in Microsoft 365. New customer tenants are counted under both Enterprise and SMB tracks; partner is awarded points using the track that yields the higher total score.
MAX_POINTS: 20
THRESHOLDS_BY_TRACK: **Enterprise track** – 4 points per net-new customer, up to 5 customers (20 points). **SMB track** – 2 points per net-new customer, up to 10 customers (20 points).
CALCULATION: Net customer adds = (total qualifying customer tenants at end of last month) − (total qualifying customer tenants 12 months prior).
ELIGIBLE_CUSTOMER_CRITERIA: Enterprise – each customer tenant with ≥ 1 workload **with > 300 paid licenses**. SMB – each customer tenant with ≥ 1 workload **with 11–300 paid licenses**. (If a tenant crosses above 300 licenses but was SMB in previous year, it remains eligible for SMB track scoring for one year.)
ELIGIBLE_WORKLOADS: (Applies to both tracks) Exchange Online; Intune; Microsoft 365 Copilot; Microsoft 365 Apps; Microsoft Entra ID P1; SharePoint Online; Teams; Teams Apps; Teams for Frontline Workers; Teams Meetings; Teams Phone; Teams Phone – Calling; Teams Phone – Enabled Users; Teams Rooms (MAU); Viva Connections; Viva Engage; Viva Goals; Viva Insights; Viva Learning; Viva Topics; Windows 365.
ELIGIBLE_ASSOCIATION_TYPES: **Enterprise:** Claiming Partner of Record (CPOR); Digital Partner of Record (DPOR). **SMB:** CPOR; Cloud Solution Provider (CSP) (Tier 1, Tier 2).
METRIC_NAME: Skilling — Intermediate Certifications
DESCRIPTION: Measures the number of individuals with eligible intermediate (associate-level) Microsoft 365 certifications.
MAX_POINTS: 10
THRESHOLDS_BY_TRACK: **Enterprise track:** 4 certified individuals = 10 points. **SMB track:** 2 certified individuals = 10 points.
NOTE: Each certified individual counts once (multiple certs by the same person still count as one person).
ELIGIBLE_CERTIFICATIONS: Microsoft 365 Fundamentals; Microsoft 365 Certified: Teams Application Developer Associate; Microsoft 365 Certified: Teams Administrator Associate; Microsoft 365 Certified: Messaging Administrator Associate; Microsoft Certified: Identity and Access Administrator Associate; Microsoft 365 Certified: Collaboration Communications Systems Engineer Associate; Microsoft 365 Certified: Endpoint Administrator Associate.
METRIC_NAME: Skilling — Advanced Certifications
DESCRIPTION: Measures the number of individuals with eligible advanced (expert-level or technical assessment) Microsoft 365 certifications.
MAX_POINTS: 15
THRESHOLDS_BY_TRACK: **Enterprise track:** 2 certified individuals = 15 points. **SMB track:** 1 certified individual = 15 points.
ELIGIBLE_CERTIFICATIONS: Microsoft 365 Certified: Enterprise Administrator Expert; Teams Meetings and Meeting Rooms Technical Assessment.
METRIC_NAME: Customer Success — Usage Growth
DESCRIPTION: Measures growth in **Monthly Active Users (MAU)** across eligible Microsoft 365 workloads over the last year.
MAX_POINTS: 30
CALCULATION: Usage growth = (eligible active users last month) − (eligible active users 12 months ago).
THRESHOLDS_BY_TRACK: **Enterprise track (association type):** CPOR – 1,000 MAU increase for 30 points; DPOR – 4,000 MAU increase for 30 points. **SMB track (association type):** CPOR – 500 MAU increase for 30 points; CSP – 2,000 MAU increase for 30 points.
NOTE: The partner is scored on whichever association type yields a higher score (points are applied proportional to actual growth up to the maximum).
METRIC_NAME: Customer Success — Deployments
DESCRIPTION: Measures net new deployments of Microsoft 365 workloads with at least 40% active usage across paid licenses in eligible customer tenants (last month vs. 12 months prior).
MAX_POINTS: 25
CALCULATION: Net new deployments = (eligible deployments last month) − (eligible deployments 12 months ago). (Deployments claimed within the last 12 months that were already above 40% active are excluded.)
THRESHOLDS_BY_TRACK: **Enterprise track (association type):** CPOR – 5 points per new deployment; 5 net new deployments = 25 points. **SMB track:** CPOR – 2.5 points per new deployment; 10 net new deployments = 25 points.
NOTE: Points are awarded under the association type (CPOR vs. DPOR/CSP) that yields more points, up to 25 maximum total. Partial credit is given if fewer deployments are achieved.
SPECIALIZATIONS (Optional): Advanced specializations include **Adoption and Change Management**, **Calling for Microsoft Teams**, **Meetings and Meeting Rooms for Microsoft Teams**, **Teamwork Deployment** (a maximum of 3 Modern Work specializations can receive benefits).

RENEWAL_RULES_AND_MAINTENANCE:
- **ANNUAL_RENEWAL:** A Solutions Partner designation must be renewed each year by again achieving ≥ 70 points (with no zero-point metrics) during the designated 6-month time window (as defined in QUALIFICATION_WINDOW above). If the renewal threshold is not met by the anniversary date + 30 days, the designation will expire (requiring a new purchase to re-enroll).
- **NO_ZERO_METRICS:** The requirement to maintain >0 points in every metric is continuous. If any metric drops to 0 (even if the total remains ≥ 70), the partner will lose the designation until the metric is improved.
- **CERTIFICATION_RETIREMENTS:** Microsoft may retire or replace certifications. e.g., *Microsoft Information Protection Administrator* retires on May 31, 2025, but will continue to count toward Security skilling points for one year if earned before retirement. Partners should plan certification attainment to pre-empt retirements and maintain their Skilling points.
- **GLOBAL_CURRENCY:** All revenue and consumption thresholds (e.g., ACR, revenue, MAU counts) are denominated in U.S. Dollars (USD) and standardized worldwide.
